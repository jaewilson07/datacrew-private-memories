## Top Pain Points

1. **AI Agent names are product claims without product proof** — "CFO AI Agent", "DOT AI Agent" etc. have zero case studies. A CFO Googling "CFO AI agent" finds OpenAI, Anthropic, and 20 VC-backed startups before FreshBI.
2. **$7K/month is in no-man's-land without the right comparison frame** — website compares against EY; actual buyer compares against hiring a Power BI analyst at $90-120K/year
3. **1,000 clients and no aggregate proof** — enough data to say "manufacturing clients reduce reporting lag from 5 days to same-day within 90 days" but they don't. Vidi Corp uses volume proof aggressively.
4. **ERP silence costs first calls** — mid-market logistics and manufacturing run GP/NAV/BC/Sage. FreshBI's website mentions zero ERP systems by name. Tigunia explicitly leads with Dynamics ERP + BI.
5. **Trustpilot has 2 reviews** — with 1,000+ clients, 2 reviews is a social proof gap
