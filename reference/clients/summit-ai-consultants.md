## Top Pain Points

1. **Defensive framing = commodity awareness** — "We don't offer generic tech support or vague AI readiness audits" / "No consulting theater." This language only appears when losing deals to that perception.
2. **Six service lines for a small team = no clear ICP** — each service line is a different buyer persona and sales motion
3. **Logo credibility mismatch** — QSR logos likely come from pre-Summit ventures. Site implies current relationship.
4. **Lead magnet converting wrong buyers** — 58-page PDF workbook attracts learners, not buyers. No pricing, no case studies by vertical, no qualification gate.
5. **Blog content disconnected from sales** — posts cover frontier AI trends (Claude Mythos, AI layoffs, OpenClaw) — thought leadership for other consultants, not for the CFO writing the check.
