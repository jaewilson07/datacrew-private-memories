## Industry Snapshot

Realist AI is a 100% GCP-exclusive AI consultancy (Littleton, CO + Edmonton, AB) founded by Ryan den Otter in 2016. Google Cloud Partner Advantage + Google Cloud Analytics Distinction credentials. Productized funnel: Pathfinder → Strategy Workshop → POC → Ongoing cloud support. Named products: The Pathfinder, The Strategy Workshop, The POC, Cloud Consumption & Support, AI Agents, Gemini Enterprise, Financing. VERY HIGH fragmentation — GCP partner directory has hundreds of boutiques; competes against SADA, 66degrees, Searce, and Google's own PSO.

**Key differentiator buried:** Three-step productized funnel (Pathfinder → Workshop → POC) is smarter than 90% of GCP boutiques. But Wix JavaScript means Google crawls a contact form, not the product. And financing is a category moat buried as a menu item.

## Top Pain Points

1. **Ryan is the product AND delivery AND CEO** — title says "Founder, CEO, Sr. Data Engineer & Lead Solutions Architect." Fortune 500 procurement teams won't sign a $150K SOW if one person is the single point of failure.
2. **Three-step funnel is the right idea, rendered invisible** — Wix JavaScript means Google crawls a contact form. A prospect Googling "GCP AI POC consulting" finds SADA, not Realist.
3. **"Fortune 500 and Global 2000" with zero named clients or logos** — press release mentions it; website shows only a contact form. Either true (massive missed proof) or aspirational (credibility risk).
4. **100% GCP commitment = competing against GCP's own PSO** — without a vertical or use-case niche, Realist competes on price against firms with 100x the headcount.
5. **Financing is buried as a menu item** — offering to finance a GCP consulting engagement is genuinely unusual and a deal-unlocker. One line in a Wix nav menu with no explanation.
