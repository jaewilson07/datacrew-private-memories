## Domo Relationship

**NLC Mutual is a major Domo customer** — they built Mutual Insights, a Domo-powered analytics platform for their member pools.

- **Product**: Mutual Insights — Domo BI platform for member pool data aggregation, benchmarking, and visualization
- **Domo Everywhere**: Members use Domo Everywhere to self-serve claims history
- **Domo Publish**: Aggregates data from 24 state pools into a unified national view for benchmarking
- **Domo.AI**: Using AI internally to connect to third-party data (NOAA hurricane data, property overlays)
- **Customer story**: Featured on domo.com/customers/nlcmutual
- **Revenue impact**: "Helped us grow our own revenue by 50 percent over the last five years" — Mark Snodgrass
- **Efficiency**: "Reports that used to take six to eight weeks are now done in a day, sometimes within an hour"
