## What They Do

InformData provides **verifiable people data** via real-time APIs and integrations for background screening, onboarding, and continuous workforce monitoring. They serve CRAs (Consumer Reporting Agencies), employers, insurers, and platforms.

Core offerings:

- **Criminal background checks** — 97% US population coverage, 92% of US courts
- **Driving records (MVR/CDLIS)** — commercial and noncommercial
- **Employment/education verification** — identity and credential checks
- **Monitoring product suite** (launched June 2025) — real-time alerts refreshed every 60 seconds, behavioral insights, identity confidence scores
- **Direct-Source API** — seamless integration with HRIS, ATS, compliance systems
- **MyWay Results** — proprietary formatting technology for customized result delivery

## Domo Usage (Deep Dive)

InformData is a **flagship Domo customer** with a published customer story and a 2025 Ovation Award winner.

### Business Challenge

After a merger (acquired Sentient Information Systems Jan 2024, Sentinel Information Systems May 2024), InformData faced multiple systems and divergent KPIs. They needed a unified data culture.

### Domo Implementation

- **Centralized finance data** — payroll analytics with drill-down by department and payroll level
- **Month-end close** — shortened by 2-3 days per month
- **Profitability tracking** — pricing, resource allocation, business health metrics
- **Self-serve analytics** — non-technical users access data without relying on data engineering team

### DAISY (Data Analytics and Intelligence System)

Marcus Wilkins built **DAISY**, an agentic AI chatbot on Domo.AI:

- Users ask questions in plain English — DAISY interprets, builds queries against secure schema, returns answers
- **AI never touches underlying data** — answers as if given the data, but never sees it
- Pattern detection — identifies recurring questions to inspire new dashboards and apps
- Evolved from chatbot to **self-reviewing intelligence network** (Domopalooza 2026 session)
- Uses Domo Code Engine, Workflows, Agents, FileSets, Pro Code Editor
