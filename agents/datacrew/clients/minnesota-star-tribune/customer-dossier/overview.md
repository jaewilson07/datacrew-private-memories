## Why It Matters

The Star Tribune is the **largest news organization in the upper Midwest** and one of the highest-circulation daily newspapers in the U.S. They are in the middle of an aggressive digital transformation led by CEO Steve Grove (ex-Google/YouTube) and CPO Aron Pilhofer. They built a data warehouse + CDP, moved from PHP to Next.js/React, and are actively using **Domo + Snowflake** as their analytics stack. Matt Smigielski leads the Domo platform — he's a Domo Connections Tour 2025 speaker.

## Domo Usage

- **Platform**: Domo + Snowflake (Snowflake powers Domo datasets)
- **Admin**: Matt Smigielski leads development and administration of Domo
- **Use case**: Insights driving strategic decisions across the organization
- **Data workflows**: End-to-end ownership — from Snowflake ingestion to Domo dashboards
- **Domo Community**: Matt is active as **MattTheGuru** (1 post, 78 comments)
- **Connections Tour 2025**: Speaker at Minneapolis stop (Sep 9, 2025)

## Digital Transformation Context

- **CEO**: Steve Grove (ex-Google, ex-YouTube, ex-MN Dept of Employment & Economic Development) — hired Apr 2023
- **CPO**: Aron Pilhofer — first Chief Product Officer, leading digital transformation
- **Goal**: 200% increase in digital subscriptions over 5 years
- **Tech stack overhaul**: Replaced legacy PHP framework with Next.js/React; Arc XP as central content hub
- **Data infrastructure**: Building own data warehouse + CDP (Customer Data Platform)
- **Supporting tools**: Norkon, Pugpig, Piano (subscription management)
- **Rebrand**: Refreshed name, visual identity, website, tagline (2024) — partnered with Colle McVoy and Code and Theory
- **AI integrations**: Exploring AI, affiliate marketing, experiential activations

## Star Tribune Data Analytics Services

The Star Tribune also sells **data analytics as a service** to advertisers through their Advantage Digital Services division:

- Proprietary Publisher Data Management Platform
- 8 million unique monthly visitors to StarTribune.com
- Audience segmentation (1.4M white-collar professionals, 1.3M affluent adults, 700K young adults, etc.)
- Core audience KPIs, engagement optimization, online/offline measurement
- Dedicated analytics team per client

## Business Context

- **Legacy media under pressure**: Declining print revenue, slowing digital subscription growth, competition from TikTok/Netflix
- **COVID-19 data journalism**: Built complex scrapers (thousands of lines of code) for MN COVID-19 tracker — most-visited article in StarTribune.com history. Moved to weekly updates Jul 2021.
- **Foundry425**: In-house creative/content agency (founded 2021) creating concepts for Star Tribune's best clients
- **Meta Journalism Project**: Received $350K grant (Feb 2023)
