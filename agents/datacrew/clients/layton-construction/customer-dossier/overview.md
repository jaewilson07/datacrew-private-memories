### Domo Use Cases (from CEO David Layton's 2019 presentation)

- **Construction site heatmaps** — visualize site status by state and time, "know what's happening without going to the site"
- **Safety management** — weather-related risk analysis (e.g., higher slip/fall rates on rainy days), drill-down by division/project for fact-based safety directives
- **Real-time progress dashboards** — clients see live construction progress (25,000 sq ft warehouse, 12-month build)
- **Predictive alerts** — automated alerts when progress deviates from plan, replacing days-to-weeks reporting lag
- **Employee utilization KPIs** — hackathon-winning project correlating variables with utilization

## Company Profile

Layton Construction is a nationally-ranked commercial contractor that drives performance and delivers predictability. Part of the **STO Building Group** — a family of 14 builders with a 5,700-person platform sharing data, lessons learned, trade relationships, bonding, and buying power.
