## Company Profile

GE HealthCare is a leading global medical technology and digital solutions innovator. Enables clinicians to make faster, more informed decisions through intelligent devices, data analytics, applications and services, supported by its Edison intelligence platform.

### Key Products

- **Edison Intelligence Platform** — AI-powered data analytics
- **GE HealthCare Command Center** — AI-driven hospital operations
- **Digital Twin** — Predictive modeling for hospital operations
- **Centricity suite** — PACS, Perinatal, Anesthesia, Critical Care, Opera, Imaging Analytics, Insights
- Ultrasound, MRI, CT, Interventional imaging
- Pharmaceutical Diagnostics

### Tech Stack

Domo, Spotfire, Oracle EBS, Hyperion (Essbase, Planning), Cognos, OBIEE, PeopleSoft, Salesforce, Power BI, Snowflake, Alteryx, Power Automate, Smartsheet, NextGen

### Recent Acquisitions

- icometrix (imaging AI)
- Intelerad Medical Systems (cloud-enabled enterprise imaging)
- Nihon Medi-Physics (radiopharmaceuticals)
