## **Job Description**

Allied Universal® is hiring an FP&A Manager. The FP&A Manager is responsible for playing a key role as a strategic business partner in supporting the annual planning process, quarterly forecasting, and monthly/weekly management reporting and analysis for the consolidated company results. The FP&A Manager will report to the Director of Corporate Finance, as well as work closely with the regional financial leaders to help in delivering the financial targets for the company.

**RESPONSIBILITIES:**

- Prepare monthly financial closing packages
- Support continued development of 5-year plan operating model (IS, BS, CF)
- Develop annual operating plan to interface with the field organization to help optimize business needs
- Assist In the preparation of annual cash flow budget, including analyzing historical trends, forecasting future cash needs, and ensuring alignment with organization financial goals
- Develop world class financial reporting and profitability analyses to provide executive management with transparent insights and decision support that drive sales and profits higher
- Consolidate financials across multiple regions and businesses while collaborating with divisional controllers to ensure accuracy and alignment
- Drive cross-functional projects and process improvements to enhance EBITDA and develop a best-in-class finance department
- Work with financial sponsors to support capital raising
