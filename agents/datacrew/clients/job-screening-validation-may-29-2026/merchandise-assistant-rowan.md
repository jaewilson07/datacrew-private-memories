## Description

We’ve built Rowan by rethinking the ear piercing experience from the ground up—prioritizing safety, clinical expertise, and a customer experience that feels both celebratory and trusted. Every piercing is performed by licensed nurses and guided by our Rowan Clinical Piercing Standard® and paired with our hypoallergenic products, setting a clear standard that is differentiated from traditional retail models. Our goal is to create a destination where customers feel confident, cared for, and excited to participate.

The Merchandise Assistant supports the execution of Rowan’s jewelry and ear piercing assortment

strategy through strong organization, reporting, sample management, and cross-functional coordination.

This role reports directly to the CSCO and plays a critical role in supporting product launches, assortment

updates, inventory tracking, vendor communication, and merchandising operations.

The ideal candidate is highly detail-oriented, organized, analytical, and passionate about fashion, jewelry,

and customer experience. This individual will help ensure smooth execution across vendor product

development, merchandising, studio communication, and assortment management while gaining

exposure to buying and product strategy.
