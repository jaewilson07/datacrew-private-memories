**Operations Coordinator**

The Operations Coordinator is responsible for the effective and accurate recording and collection of operational Key Performance Indicators including daily data analysis and tracking of efficiency progress, downtime, scrap, changeover, and data trending. This position will assist in looking into areas to improve cost efficiency. In addition, this position will work with others in various departments to achieve factory business objectives of safety, efficiency, customer service, quality, GMP’s, cost, FP&R, and environmental protection.
