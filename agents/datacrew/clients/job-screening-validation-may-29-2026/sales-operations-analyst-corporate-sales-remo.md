CrowdStrike is seeking a Sales Operations Support Analyst to join our AMS

Corporate Sales Strategy & Operations team. This role will directly support the

Senior Sales Operations Analysts in delivering operational excellence to our

AMS Sales organization. The successful candidate will assist with data

management, reporting, and process implementation to enable the team

to drive strategic initiatives. This position offers an excellent opportunity to

develop your skills and grow your career at CrowdStrike.

**What You'll Do:**

Provide Direct Support to Senior Sales Operations Analysts:**

- Assist with data collection, cleansing, and organization to support senior analysts' strategic projects
- Help implement and maintain sales processes designed by the team
- Collaborate with senior analysts to ensure timely delivery of insights to sale leadership

Support Sales Performance Tracking:

- Help maintain dashboards and reports that track key sales metrics
- Assist with data validation to ensure accuracy in performance reporting
- Support the senior team in identifying trends and anomalies in sales
