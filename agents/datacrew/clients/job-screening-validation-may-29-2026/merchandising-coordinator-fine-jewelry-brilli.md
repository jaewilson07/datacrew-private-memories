**Merchandising Coordinator, Fine Jewelry Merchandising** **- Remote**

**Position Overview:**

We are looking for a detail-oriented Merchandising Coordinator to join our Merchandising team and support the growth of our Fine Jewelry business. In this role, you will support product lifecycle and launch initiatives across cross-functional teams — including Design, Production, Planning, Ecommerce, and Marketing — ensuring a seamless product experience across all customer touchpoints.

- Conduct competitor reviews to monitor trends, pricing, promotional activity, and product introductions.

- Maintain accurate SKU attribute and pricing information.
- Collaborate with cross functional teams, including production, operations, marketing, and site merchandising to launch new product and drive category performance.
