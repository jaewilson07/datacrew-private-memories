## Description

**PRIMARY FUNCTION:**

The Senior Manager of Digital and Performance Marketing at Zippo Manufacturing Company/W.R. Case & Sons Cutlery will lead marketing initiatives across our portfolio of iconic American brands. This role owns the strategy, execution, and optimization of paid media (paid social, pay-per-click, and programmatic) along with email and SMS marketing and will be responsible for driving scalable customer acquisition and revenue growth, while also leveraging email as a high-impact owned channel for engagement and conversion. The role will oversee three staff and reports to the Vice President of Global Marketing.

The Senior Manager leads a high-impact function, and team, at the intersection of media, data, and creative—building a modern growth engine that balances brand storytelling with measurable performance outcomes.
