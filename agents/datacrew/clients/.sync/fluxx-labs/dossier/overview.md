## Domo Relationship

**Fluxx is a Domo Embed partner** — they sell Grantelligence, a Domo-powered analytics add-on to their grants management platform.

- **Product**: Grantelligence — embedded BI for grantmakers, built on Domo Embed
- **Launch**: 2023 (30% beta conversion, 50% of new customers include at purchase)
- **Use Case**: Pre-built dashboards for grant requests, payments, compliance, staff workload
- **Tech Stack**: Domo Embed, Fluxx Data Core, CSV uploads, Excel-like formulas
- **Quote**: "Domo provides the insights required to help the people giving away money to do it faster and with more accuracy." — Tammy Marinac, Director of Product Marketing
