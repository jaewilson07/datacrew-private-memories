# GE HealthCare — Overview


**Industry:** Medical Technology / Digital Health Solutions

**Headquarters:** Chicago, Illinois, United States

**Founded:** 1892

**Size:** 36,579 employees

**Revenue:** $20.6B

**Ticker:** GEHC

**Website:** [https://gehealthcare.com/](https://gehealthcare.com/)


**Description:** GE HealthCare (NASDAQ: GEHC) is a leading global medical technology and digital solutions innovator. Enables clinicians to make faster, more informed decisions through intelligent devices, data analytics, applications and services, supported by its Edison intelligence platform. 36,579 employees, $20.6B annual revenue, operates in 126 countries.


## Domo Usage


Confirmed major Domo customer. Multiple MajorDomo-certified practitioners across the organization. Domo used for: (1) Global Cash Regional Teams visualization (rollout led by Mercedes Rowe), (2) Commercial Team BI platform initiatives and data governance (Linda Fligg), (3) International analytics and KPI dashboards (Shamil Salam), (4) Profitability dashboards (Emmanuelle Phan, >$100k savings), (5) FP&A reporting (Molly Young). At least 8 MajorDomo-certified practitioners identified.
