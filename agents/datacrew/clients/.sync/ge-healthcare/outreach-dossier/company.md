## Pain Points

- Disconnected BI tools created data bottlenecks and slowed reporting across global finance teams.
- Dashboards took too long to build and lacked the scale needed for mass consumption across business units.
- Finance lacked a single source of truth, making consistent global data sharing difficult.

## Outreach Angle

GE HealthCare is already a high-adoption Domo customer with global finance buy-in — the opportunity is to deepen usage into other business units (e.g., professional services, healthcare IT) and position Domopalooza as the venue to benchmark their data strategy against peers.
