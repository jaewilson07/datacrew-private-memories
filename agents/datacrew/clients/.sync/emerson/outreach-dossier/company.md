## Domo Signals

**Domo usage:** Emerson's Cold Chain business uses Domo to track and verify temperatures across the global perishable goods supply chain, with customer-facing dashboards built via Domo Everywhere; automated dataflow engine saved hundreds of hours of manual work across 1,150+ sensors.

## Pain Points

- Emerson's Cold Chain division lacked a scalable way to capture and share real-time data from refrigeration products and IoT sensors across the perishable supply chain.
- Manual data processing consumed hundreds of engineering hours before Domo automation was introduced.
- Customers needed self-serve visibility into temperature compliance data, requiring Emerson to build externally facing analytics.

## Outreach Angle

Emerson has already proven Domo's value internally and with customers through Cold Chain supply chain dashboards—there's a strong opportunity to expand that footprint into other divisions (e.g., Power & Water, Process Automation) that are now investing in GenAI-enabled operations.
