# Mastercard — Customer Dossier

## Company Overview

**Industry:** Global Payments Technology / Financial Services **Website:** [Mastercard](https://www.mastercard.com) **Headquarters:** Purchase, New York **Size:** 30,000+ employees **Revenue:** $25.1B (2024) **Public:** NYSE: MA **CEO:** Michael Miebach **Founded:** 1966

Mastercard is a global technology company in the payments industry. It operates the world's fastest payments processing network, connecting consumers, financial institutions, merchants, governments, and businesses in more than 210 countries and territories. Mastercard's Data & Services division provides analytics, insights, and consulting solutions.
