## Pain Points

- Rapid growth created disconnected billing and customer management systems, making it difficult to leverage data for timely decisions.
- Scaling to thousands of new customers per month required intelligent, unified data access across the entire workforce to maintain service quality.
- Migrating to a new combined customer management and billing platform while maintaining operational continuity was a significant data integration challenge.
