## Domo Signals

**Domo usage:** Rising Star Outreach Uses Domo to Help Uplift People with Leprosy Across India (Domo press release, Nov 13 2025): 'Using Domo, Rising Star Outreach was able to connect its data, build dashboards, and empower staff in both India and the U.S. to make informed decisions.' Participates in the Domo for Good program with 90 active Domo users.

**Domopalooza presence:** Kristen Edwards, Program Director, delivered a Fireside Chat at Domopalooza 2026 (March 24–26, 2026) interviewed by Lacy Zimmerman of Domo, and won the 2026 Hall of Fame 'Positive Impact' Award recognizing data-driven community impact through Domo for Good.

## Pain Points

- Data was siloed and disconnected across U.S. headquarters and India field operations, preventing a unified view of program impact.
- Staff spent excessive time manually collecting numbers instead of focusing on delivering services across 100+ leprosy colonies.
- Field teams in India lacked access to real-time dashboards needed to make informed, timely programmatic decisions.

## Outreach Angle

Rising Star Outreach is one of Domo's most visible nonprofit success stories — Kristen Edwards just won the 2026 Hall of Fame Positive Impact Award and headlined Domopalooza with a fireside chat on turning messy data into mission impact. This is a highly engaged Domo champion with room to deepen adoption across 170+ staff and expand automated reporting as their India programs scale.
