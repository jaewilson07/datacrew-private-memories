## Domo Signals

**Domo usage:** Marriott co-presented a Domopalooza session titled 'Yum! + Marriott: Sharing Is Caring—Serving Our Partners with Data and a Smile' (Jan 29, 2025), focused on using embedded analytics to empower franchisees and scale data sharing globally.

**Domopalooza presence:** Charles Pollard of Marriott Digital Services delivered a Fireside Chat at Domopalooza, listed in the official Domopalooza resource library at domo.com/domopalooza/resources.

## Pain Points

- Scaling data and analytics access across a global franchise and owner network with diverse technology maturity levels.
- Integrating disparate data platforms following large-scale M&A (e.g., Starwood merger) into a unified Modern Data Technology platform.
- Empowering franchisees and owners with self-serve embedded analytics without compromising data governance or brand standards.

## Outreach Angle

Marriott has publicly demonstrated Domo's role in scaling franchisee data sharing at Domopalooza — a signal that embedded analytics at the partner layer is a strategic priority. Outreach should anchor on helping them deepen or expand that franchisee analytics program as they grow their global portfolio.
