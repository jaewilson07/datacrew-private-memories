## Pain Points

- Dealer-level analysis was previously a manual, time-intensive process requiring significant human effort to scale across hundreds of dealerships.
- Translating raw customer sentiment and VoC data into actionable dealer insights in near-real-time has been a persistent challenge.
- Enabling non-technical dealer staff and internal stakeholders to self-serve on AI and analytics outputs without deep BI expertise.
