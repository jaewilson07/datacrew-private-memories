## Pain Points

- Fragmented marketing data across 41 geographically dispersed parks made consistent BI and reporting difficult.
- Lean IT resources created pressure to scale analytics infrastructure without increasing headcount or complexity.
- Mergers (Cedar Fair + Six Flags) required maintaining workflow and app flexibility while integrating disparate data systems.
- **No DUG Slack or Domo forum presence** — the team is working in isolation from the Domo community.

## Outreach Angle

Six Flags has already proven Domo's value at scale — Lauren Ochs took that story to Domopalooza 2026. The opportunity is to deepen that partnership by expanding use cases (revenue optimization, guest experience analytics) now that the merged 41-park footprint is stabilizing. The post-Cedar Fair merger creates massive data consolidation work — consulting opportunity on Domo expertise for the marketing analytics team + data consolidation from merger.
