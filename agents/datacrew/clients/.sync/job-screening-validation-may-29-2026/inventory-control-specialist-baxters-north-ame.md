Monitors and updates master schedule to reflect inventory turnover, stocking decisions, forecasting, and administrative controls. Maintains and updates inventory system. Creates and reviews reports to identify trends or issues and to support or initiate actions or adjustments.

Ensures data integrity processes are defined and followed within manufacturing, product management, supply chain and financial systems.
