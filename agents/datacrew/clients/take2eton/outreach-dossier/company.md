## Domo Signals

**Domo usage:** Domo unified Take2Eton's four core systems across travel, finance, operations, and customer data to create a single, real-time view across the business. Data onboarding through APIs is now 90 percent faster, and automated alerts flag potential issues the moment they appear. (BusinessWire / Domo press release, Jan 14 2026)

**Domopalooza presence:** Take2Eton CTO Rob Cope presented at Domopalooza 2026 (March 24–26, Salt Lake City). LinkedIn post confirms: 'Really enjoyed presenting yesterday on behalf of Take2Eton Group to showcase how Domo has helped us grow our data and AI offering to our customers. #DP26'

## Pain Points

- Data was siloed across four core systems (travel, finance, operations, customer) with no unified real-time view, slowing decision-making.
- Client reporting was manual and slow — insights that now take seconds previously took days to produce.
- Data onboarding was time-intensive; API-based ingestion was not automated, creating operational bottlenecks.

## Outreach Angle

Take2Eton has publicly showcased Domo as the backbone of their 'Lens' BI product and Rob Cope just headlined Domopalooza 2026 — they're a vocal Domo advocate with a data-forward CTO. An outreach angle focused on deepening the Domo AI/data products footprint or co-marketing their success story would land well given their demonstrated appetite for innovation.
