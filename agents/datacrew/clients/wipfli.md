## Company Overview

Wipfli is a top 25 US accounting and advisory firm serving mid-market organizations. With 90+ years of history, they've grown through 34 acquisitions over the past decade, tripling in size. They serve 54,000+ clients across diverse industries.

**Structure:** Alternative practice structure — Wipfli LLP (licensed CPA firm, attest services) and Wipfli Advisory LLC (tax and business consulting, not a licensed CPA firm). The "Wipfli" brand covers both entities.

**PE Investment (Aug 2025):** New Mountain Capital (~$55B AUM) acquired a ~40% minority stake through its Strategic Equity program (non-control investments). Valuation north of $1B. Purpose: accelerate growth, invest in talent/technology, fuel acquisitions. Firm remains majority-owned and partner-led.
