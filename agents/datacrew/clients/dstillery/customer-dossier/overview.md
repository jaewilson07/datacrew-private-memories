## What Dstillery Does

Dstillery is an AI-powered audience targeting company for programmatic advertising. They use multimodal AI to analyze 100B+ daily events and create custom audience segments for advertisers. Their technology replaces cookie-based targeting with ID-free Custom AI and Predictive Contextual solutions. They serve both agencies and brands directly through their Data Marketplace and integration partnerships with platforms like Microsoft Advertising, The Trade Desk, and Google DV360.

### Key Products


| Product | Purpose |
| --- | --- |
| Custom AI Audiences | AI-built audience segments from behavioral and contextual signals |
| ID-free Custom AI | Privacy-safe audience targeting without cookies or device IDs |
| Predictive Contextual | Context-aware ad targeting that predicts user intent from page content; integrated with Microsoft Advertising (April 2025) |
| Custom Search Lookalikes | Lookalike modeling from search behavior data |
| Audience Studio | Self-service platform for building and activating custom audiences |
| Data Marketplace | Off-the-shelf audience segments accessible via major DSPs (The Trade Desk, Google DV360, Xandr) |
