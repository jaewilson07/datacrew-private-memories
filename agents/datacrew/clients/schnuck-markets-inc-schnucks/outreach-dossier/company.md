## Domo Signals

**Domo usage:** Schnucks is leveraging the Domo AI and Data Products platform to revolutionize its approach to grocery retail data and analytics. Store teams use Domo daily to optimize staffing, production, and customer service, while leaders rely on it to track strategic progress across multiple states. The BI team built interactive dashboards via Domo's App Studio and low-code app development tools.

## Pain Points

- Fragmented, siloed data across stores and departments made it difficult to get a unified, real-time view of operations.
- Store-level decision-making on staffing, production, and customer service lacked timely, actionable data.
- Executive and strategic alignment across a large multi-state footprint required consistent, accessible reporting.

## Outreach Angle

Schnucks has publicly committed to Domo as their AI and data operations platform, with their CDIO leading the charge on unifying retail data across 113 stores — making them an ideal conversation partner for anything that extends or deepens that investment. Referencing their December 2025 press release and Tom Henry's promotion signals genuine executive buy-in worth building on.
