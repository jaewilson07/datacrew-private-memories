## Pain Points

- Managing and forecasting viewership data at scale across broadcast and streaming platforms requires fast, accurate analytics.
- Empowering distributed teams (30 franchises) with consistent, human-guided data-driven decision-making is operationally complex.
- Optimizing live streaming performance in real time demands low-latency data pipelines and actionable dashboards.

## Outreach Angle

The NBA is already deep in their Domo journey — from basic reporting to AI-powered viewership forecasting — as showcased at Domopalooza 2025. There's a clear opportunity to expand on that AI foundation, whether through additional Domo apps, advanced AI agents, or cross-team data democratization across all 30 franchises.
