## Business Model

Blue Raven provides supply chain solutions across the full platform lifecycle — from acquisition to sustainment — for military and commercial customers. Their core value proposition is keeping fleets operational, ready, and safe through:

- **Distribution** — OEM parts sourcing and delivery to military and commercial end users
- **Program Management** — end-to-end supply chain orchestration for long-life military programs
- **Obsolescence Management** — proactive parts availability tracking and sourcing
- **Counterfeit Avoidance** — quality assurance and authentication for critical components
- **Kitting** — assembly and packaging of component kits for specific platforms
- **Engineering Services** — technical support for mission-critical components and systems

**Key differentiator:** SEDNA Analytics — a proprietary data analytics platform that aggregates data from multiple sources (proprietary collection software, public data, paid services) to deliver market intelligence, competitive analysis, and demand forecasting.

## SEDNA Analytics Platform

SEDNA is Blue Raven's proprietary supply chain intelligence suite. It is the company's primary technology differentiator and the data product that David C. supports as Director of Data Analytics.

**Capabilities:**

- Competitive analysis and market share reporting
