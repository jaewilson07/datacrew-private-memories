## Domo Signals

**Domo usage:** ESPN uses Domo to tap into viewer sentiment so it can maintain its reputation as the Worldwide Leader in Sports. ESPN's entire customer care team uses the VoCx application built on Domo. ESPN also uses Domo App Studio to enhance the fan experience (Domo press release, May 2024).

**Domopalooza presence:** ESPN and OneMagnify presented a case study at Domopalooza 2026 titled 'AI Data Storytelling: An ESPN & OneMagnify Case Study — From CRM to C-Suite: Data Storytelling & Executive Intelligence' (published March 31, 2026 on the Domopalooza resources page).

## Pain Points

- Tracking and actioning customer sentiment at scale across live sporting events, app interactions, and multiple digital platforms in near real-time.
- Translating raw fan feedback and CRM data into concise, AI-generated executive narratives that reach the C-suite without manual reporting overhead.
- Monitoring and improving app performance across a massive subscriber base spanning ESPN+, the flagship app, and seven linear TV networks.

## Outreach Angle

ESPN is a power Domo customer that just showcased an AI-driven executive intelligence solution at Domopalooza 2026 — a signal they are actively pushing Domo deeper into leadership workflows. There is a clear opening to discuss how Domo can further connect fan-sentiment data to revenue and retention decisions across their direct-to-consumer properties.
