### Consulting Opportunity

**Domo.AI + App Catalyst adoption** — Chris liked the App Catalyst announcement at DP26. Natural next step for their AI journey.

**Governance at 600+ user scale** — entire workforce on Domo means governance, permissions, and data quality are critical.

**AWS + Domo integration** — their stack includes AWS (DynamoDB, AI/ML) + Domo. Integration patterns and optimization.

**UK market** — less Domo consultant competition in the UK. First-mover advantage.

**Post-growth optimization** — 28 new systems in ~22 months means technical debt accumulation. Optimization and consolidation consulting.
