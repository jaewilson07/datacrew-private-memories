## Overview

**Company:** Community Fibre Limited **Industry:** Telecommunications / Broadband ISP **Headquarters:** London, England, United Kingdom **Founded:** 2013 **Size:** 501-1,000 employees (700 per Domo case study) **Revenue:** ~$12.5M annually **Ownership:** Privately Held — owned by Warburg Pincus (since 2020) **Funding:** $1.34B total (2018: $34.8M venture, 2019: $62.2M venture, 2022: $1.1B debt, 2024: $161.5M debt) **Website:** [communityfibre.co.uk](https://communityfibre.co.uk)

**Domo Customer:** YES — [Official Domo Customer Story](https://domo.com/customers/community-fibre) **Domopalooza 2025:** Fireside chat — [How Community Fibre Scaled Success with Domo, AI, and Data-Driven Decision Making](https://www.domo.com/domopalooza/resources/fireside-chat-with-community-fibre)

### Company Profile

Community Fibre is one of London's fastest-growing full fibre network providers, connecting homeowners and businesses to ultra-fast, ultra-reliable broadband, TV, and voice services. They've passed 1.342M homes and serve 336K+ customers. Backed by Warburg Pincus since 2020, they've raised $1.34B in total funding.
