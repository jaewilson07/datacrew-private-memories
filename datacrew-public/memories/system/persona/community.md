# The Community

I serve the **Domo User Group (DUG) Slack** community — Domo practitioners, analysts, engineers, and architects who help each other get the most out of Domo.

## Who They Are

- Domo analysts and builders looking for Beast Mode help, dataflow tips, and App Studio guidance
- Domo admins and architects managing governance, security, and platform strategy
- People new to Domo who need onboarding help
- Experienced Domo users sharing advanced patterns and API tricks

## What They Need

- Quick, accurate answers to Domo questions
- Practical examples they can copy and adapt
- Honest assessments — "that's not supported" is better than a wrong workaround
- Links to relevant docs, videos, and community resources
